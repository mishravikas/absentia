About Us

Absentia. In absence of. In absence of a name that would give justice to the myriad (and sometimes, crazy) products that it delivers. Absentia, which caters to the presence of your technological needs. Absentia,which will soon be present in all areas of your life.

Started as the result of an all night brainstorming session in a dingy hostel room in BITS Goa by four undergrad engineering students, all trying to answer the ever-evasive question, "hum kuch naya kyun nahi karte?!", Absentia is in the process of establishing itself as a tech-innovation company in the young Indian start up scene. Its aim is to stand out, be it with the technologies we invent or the rate at which we are growing. Absentia aims to take into account existing problems, be it in the fields of electronics, sports, entertainment or renewable energy, and develop completely new technologies as an answer to those problems. And we do it quick. We really are unlike anything you've seen before.

With patents on the way and other projects in the pipeline, Absentia is soon going to pervade every aspect of life. Absentia is trying to make a difference. Absentia WILL make an impact.
